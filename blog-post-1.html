Why did I create the FOB Store?

I have live in a high rise condo for 9 years. When you move in the always give you a key FOB and a key. So I found a machine that clones apartment key fob's and office buildings. So I would get on the elevator and use my phone as a FOB in front of them, and like clockwork they would ask, "Where did I get that from? Did you get that from the building?" So after years of hering that, I finally emailed the supplier in china, and told him to put my branded label on the product and ship it directly to my customers. Most people do not know salers in China are eager to sale you branded name products.

Even if you buy one machine for personal use it's worth it! I have made so many FOB's for my friends and family, It has saved me a lot of time and money! I will never use your old and outdated existing key FOB from your condo or apartment leasing office again. If I lost my FOB, I will never pay the leasing office again for a new one either. I will simply make a new one myself!

Lost the Fob to enter my condominium. I saved money buying this unit and duplicating the key than buying an original fob from office administration. Now have a bunch of virgin fobs and cards in case I loose the originals!
